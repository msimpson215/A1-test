The Details

What You're Getting

Water is pavement's worst enemy. It finds every crack, freezes, expands, and tears your lot apart from the inside. By the time you see surface damage, the problem underneath is already worse.

We use hot-pour rubberized crack filler — the professional standard. It bonds to the crack walls, flexes with temperature changes, and stays sealed through freeze-thaw cycles that destroy inferior products.

Crack filling is the best ROI in pavement maintenance. Catch it early and you're looking at a fraction of the cost of patching or replacement down the road.

How It Works

Our Process

01
Crack Survey
Walk the lot, map all cracks, identify areas needing mastic vs. standard fill.
02
Clean & Route
Blow out debris from cracks. Route wider cracks for better fill adhesion.
03
Hot-Pour Fill
Apply heated rubberized filler — flush with surface, no raised edges.
04
Cool & Seal
Allow to cool, then proceed with sealcoat if part of a full maintenance program.
