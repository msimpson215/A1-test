The Details

What You're Getting

Bollards protect storefronts, drive-throughs, utility equipment, and pedestrian areas from vehicle impact. We core drill 4" to 12" diameter holes, set steel bollards in concrete, and paint them to spec.

We handle all ADA-compliant signage posts too — accessible parking signs, speed limit signs, directional markers. One crew, one mobilization, everything done right.

Joe's crew has installed bollards at national retail chains, warehouses, and commercial properties across the Midwest. If it needs to stop a vehicle or hold a sign, we've done it.

Steel Bollards
Heavy-duty steel set in concrete. Yellow or bare steel finish. Removable options available.
Core Drilling
4" to 12" diameter through asphalt and concrete. Clean holes, proper depth every time.
ADA Signage Posts
Accessible parking, van-accessible, speed limit, and directional signs installed to spec.
Storefront Protection
Retail entrances, drive-throughs, and ATM areas protected from vehicle intrusion.
How It Works

Our Process

01
Site Assessment
Mark bollard locations, check subsurface for utilities, confirm placement meets code.
02
Core Drill
Drill clean holes to proper diameter and depth through asphalt or concrete surface.
03
Set & Pour
Set steel bollard, pour concrete surround, confirm plumb and alignment.
04
Finish & Paint
Paint to spec — yellow safety color or bare steel. Surface patched clean around base.
