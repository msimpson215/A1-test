The Details

What You're Getting

There's a reason some patches fail in one winter and others last a decade. The difference is prep. We saw-cut clean edges, remove the failed material, fix the base if needed, and compact properly. No shortcuts.

For areas that need a flexible, durable repair without full-depth removal, we use hot mastic — a rubberized asphalt compound that bonds tight and holds up under heavy traffic.

For larger sections or full lots, our paving crews run professional-grade equipment — the same LeeBoy pavers you'd see on a DOT job site.
